WITI Advisory | All Branches
In observance of Ninoy Aquino Day, as per Proclamation No. 665, there will be no classes or office transactions at all WITI Branches on August 23, 2024 (Friday).
Additionally, in observance of National Heroes Day, as per Proclamation No. 368, there will be no classes or office transactions on August 26, 2024 (Monday).
WITI Branches with a class schedule on August 24, 2024 (Saturday) will conduct online classes instead.
Regular classes and office transactions will resume on Tuesday, August 27, 2024.
