The Institution

Westbridge Institute of Technology is a university that accepts Senior High School and College students. Education offered to college students include Bachelor’s degree programs in Computer Science, Entrepreneurship, Information System, and Tourism Management. Eligible working students may also be included in a program that provides flexible class schedules and payment.
